The Renewable Energy Enterprises Foundation

We are not simply lifting one family from poverty, we are alleviating inequalities within a community.


Renewable Energy Enterprises Foundation operates within rural and impoverished communities, utilizing clean energy technologies and alternative practices, to promote health, environment, and economic mobility. 

REEF is a 501(c)(3) that engages with community stakeholders struggling with poverty.  Currently, we implement multiple strategies in effort to break down economic barriers: at the household level with the Family First Program, at the village level with the Green Community Program and at the community at large through the LEADS program.  Learn more about how you can get involved.

REEF is dedicated to accountability and transparency.  When you contribute to REEF, you know where your donation is going and how it directly benefits individuals and community stakeholders.  Learn about how you can benefit REEF.

REEF is an all-volunteer company.  We are a collaboration of highly motivated, dedicated and well-trained professionals, bringing to bear the strengths of our respective fields and areas of expertise.  Our diversity allows as to adapt to a wide range of problems and effectively address a changing world.  It’s what defines us.  Think you can help?  We'd love to hear from you.
