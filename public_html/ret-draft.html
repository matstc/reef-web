Benefits of RET

Renewable Energies Technologies (RET)

According to World Bank studies and other international monetary organizations, rural electrification has the following benefits:

  • Increase family income
  • Greater percent of children complete primary school and finish 4 year college
  • Remove source of indoor air pollution
  • Children study longer with electrical lighting
  • Fewer children

In general, parents are able to provide better healthcare and education.
